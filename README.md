Automatic Frame Extractor
This tool automatically converts PDF slides and images into high-quality 9:16 vertical frames optimized for YouTube Shorts and TikTok. To use it, simply drag and drop your PDF or image files into the upload area. You can adjust rendering quality (DPI) and sharpness in the sidebar to ensure crystal-clear text and visuals. Once processed, download all your ready-to-use video assets as a single ZIP file. It’s the perfect solution for creators looking to streamline their short-form video production workflow.
